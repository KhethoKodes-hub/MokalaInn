About Mokala Inn

A testament to empowerment and youth leadership in Northern Cape

Mokala Inn stands as a testament to empowerment and youth leadership, with a proud ownership structure of 51% black female and 49% black youth ownership. Situated in Kuruman within the Northern Cape Province, we specialize in offering top-tier accommodation and catering services, serving a diverse clientele for over a decade.

Our foundation is built on a deep-seated passion for hospitality, reflected in our commitment to creating warm and inviting spaces for our guests. Recognizing the demand for high-quality lodging and dining experiences in Kuruman, we strive to exceed expectations and provide a home-away-from-home atmosphere.

Beyond our business endeavors, Mokala Inn is driven by a broader mission of community upliftment and economic empowerment. We actively contribute to job creation, support local businesses, and promote tourism in the region.
