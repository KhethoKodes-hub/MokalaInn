Our Story

A testament to empowerment and youth leadership in Northern Cape

Mokala Inn (Pty) Ltd stands as a testament to empowerment and youth leadership, with a proud ownership structure of 51% black female and 49% black youth ownership. Situated in Kuruman within the Northern Cape Province, Mokala Inn specializes in offering top-tier accommodation and catering services, serving a diverse clientele for over a decade.

Our foundation is built on a deep-seated passion for hospitality, reflected in our commitment to creating warm and inviting spaces for our guests. Recognizing the demand for high-quality lodging and dining experiences in Kuruman, we strive to exceed expectations and provide a home-away-from-home atmosphere.

Beyond our business endeavors, Mokala Inn is driven by a broader mission of community upliftment and economic empowerment. We actively contribute to job creation, support local businesses, and promote tourism in the region, embodying values that extend far beyond the walls of our establishment.

With a steadfast dedication to excellence and a relentless focus on customer satisfaction, Mokala Inn aims to be a cornerstone of hospitality in Kuruman, enriching the lives of all who walk through our doors.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that define our purpose and direction

Our Mission

At Mokala Inn, our mission is to provide a haven of warmth, comfort, and personalized hospitality for every guest who graces our doors. Rooted in a passion for exceptional service and a commitment to creating lasting memories, we strive to be the premier choice for travelers seeking a home away from home.

With meticulous attention to detail, genuine care for our guests' needs, and an unwavering commitment to excellence, we aim to elevate the art of hospitality and exceed expectations at every turn.

Our Vision

At Mokala Inn, our vision is to be the epitome of hospitality excellence, setting the standard for unparalleled guest experiences in our region. We aspire to be recognized as more than a guest house; we aim to be a destination where the convergence of exceptional service, cultural richness, and sustainable practices creates an enduring legacy.

By seamlessly blending luxurious accommodations, authentic local experiences, and eco-friendly initiatives, we seek to redefine hospitality and leave a lasting impression on every guest who walks through our doors.
