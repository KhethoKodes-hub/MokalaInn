Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What methods of payment do you accept?
We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and EFT payments. For corporate accounts, we offer invoicing with 30-day payment terms.
Do you offer long-term stay discounts?
Yes, we offer special rates for extended stays. Please contact our reservations team for customized long-stay packages.
Can you accommodate dietary restrictions for catering?
Absolutely. Our catering team can accommodate various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and specific allergies. Please inform us in advance.
Is there WiFi available?
Yes, we provide complimentary high-speed WiFi throughout the property for all our guests.
Do you have facilities for events and conferences?
Yes, we have versatile event spaces suitable for conferences, meetings, weddings, and other special events. Our team can assist with planning and coordination.
